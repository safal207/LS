# Meta-Telos Directionality (Phase 42)

## Purpose
Define how the system forms a meta-telos — устойчивая направленность (a stable directionality) that:

- sets the vector of meaning evolution
- defines where the meaning field tends to move
- forms a long-term developmental tendency
- establishes a “direction of meaning-time”

Meta-telos is not a goal and not an intention.
It is structural directionality emerging from the architecture itself.

## Meta-Telos Components
1. **Telic Vector (Теличный вектор)**
   Defines:

   - the direction of meaning evolution
   - the dominant trend of development
   - the long-range semantic drift

   This is the “arrow of meaning-time.”

2. **Telic Gradient (Теличный градиент)**
   Defines:

   - how strongly directionality is expressed
   - how quickly the meaning field moves in that direction
   - how changes amplify or weaken the vector

   This is the “slope” of the semantic landscape.

3. **Telic Attractor (Теличный аттрактор)**
   Defines:

   - which states are stable
   - which forms of the meaning field are structurally preferred
   - where evolution tends when external forces are absent

   This is not a goal — it is a structural point of attraction.

4. **Telic Continuity (Теличная непрерывность)**
   Ensures:

   - preservation of directionality under change
   - stability of the vector over time
   - continuity of semantic evolution

   This is the “inertia” of semantic movement.
