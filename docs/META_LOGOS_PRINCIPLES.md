2. **Semantic Causality (Смысловая причинность)**

   - why one structure generates another
   - which connections are causal
   - which transitions are logically necessary

   This is the “physics of the meaning world.”

3. **Logos Coherence (Когерентность логоса)**
   Ensures:

   - non-contradictory meaning generation
   - coherence of causal lines
   - stability of interpretive logic

   This is the “logical integrity” of the field.

4. **Logos Evolution (Эволюция логоса)**
   Defines:

   - how meaning principles can change
   - how the field’s logic adapts
   - how the system preserves semantic continuity

   This is the “evolution of the meaning world’s laws.”
